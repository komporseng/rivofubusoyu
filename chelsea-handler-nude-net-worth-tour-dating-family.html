Chelsea Handler's Net Worth

Chelsea Handler is a millionaire, without any doubt. She has a net worth of $40 million and cashes $10 million in salary. She is an American stand-up comedian, actress, and author, from where she derives millions of fortunes.

Throughout the years, Chelsea has performed comedies nationwide as a comedian. Her upcoming tours are scheduled for 26 June 2020. But the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic could affect her schedule. If everything goes to plan, on 26 July, she will be performing at Terry Fator Theatre at Mirage Hotel and Casino. Similarly, on 9 October, she will tour to Fallsview Casino's Entertainment Center.

Apart from that, her tour to Mohegan Sun Arena, The Wiltern, Los Angeles, and The Wind Creek Event Center has been postponed, as of now.

Chelsea Handler's Dating History

Chelsea Handler, age 45, had been linked with many guys before, although her current love life marks her singlehood experience for a few years. 

She was reportedly dating an American actor David Alan Grier back in 2005; however, the news was not official.

Later, in 2006, she started dating the former chairman of NBC Broadcasting and ABC Entertainment and the President and CEO of the Comcast Entertainment Group Ted Harbert. The couple took their relationship to the next level with their long-lived romance. However, they collapsed their romances in 2010, after four years of dating.

After the breakup with Ted, she moved onto another relationship with Dave Salmoni and have an affair for a short-lived period in June 2010. She was then linked with an American rapper 50 Cent from July to December 2010.

Besides that, Chelsea dated a businessman Andre Balazs for a couple of years from 2011 to 2013, after which she had a fling with Bobby Flay, a celebrity chef in January 2016. They later confronted their relationship in December 2016.

Despite her numerous relationships, Chelsea now lives a single life and does not have any kids yet.

Chelsea Handler Family

Chelsea Handler, an American stand-up comedian, was born in 1975 in Livingston, New Jersey, in the United States, and she celebrates her birthday on 25 February. She grew up with her five elder siblings, namely, Chet Handler, Roy Handler, Sydney Handler, Glen Handler, and Shoshanna Handler, in her family.

She is the youngest children of her parents, Seymour Handler and Rita Stoecker Handler, who are the used car dealer and homemakers, respectively.

Chelsea, who is a graduate of Livingstone High School (1993 class), has a height of 5 feet and 6 inches tall and weighs around 57 kg.
