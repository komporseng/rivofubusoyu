Who is Francisca Lachapel? Meet This Beautiful Dominican Actress

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Francisca Lachapel is well known among the public as a Dominican actress, TV host, and beauty pageant titleholder, who has crowned Nuestra Belleza Latina on 12 April 2015. 

She was born on May 5th, 1989 in Azua de Compostela, Dominican Republic, therefore her nationality is Dominican, and holds Taurus as her birth sign.

Early Life And Family

Francisca was born and raised in Azua de Compostela, Dominican Republic by her parents with love and care but she has not revealed any sort of information regarding her parents on any social media.

Moreover, she did not face any sort of difficulties in her childhood for quality education and a good life because she is from a well-established family background.

Education Qualification

The actress of Waking Up with You is well educated and finished her early education at a private school in her hometown. After that, she got a college degree from a reputed college.

However, she has not disclosed any sort of information regarding higher education and her majors.

Career Highlights

Francisca Lachapel has made her debut on-screen career as an actress in the TV series Me caigo de risa which was released in 2014. From that, she worked in some TV series as an actress on Waking Up with You, El Fantasma de mi Novia, and Me caigo de risa. 

She even works as a co-host and host in some TV shows such as La Gran Sorpresai, ¡Despierta América!, and Se Habla USA.  

Moreover, she is currently a co-host on the popular morning show ¡Despierta América! on the Univision Network.

Is She In Married Life?

Francisca Lachapel is currently staying in married life with her husband Francesco Zampogna. The romantic couple tied their knot in 2019 and they welcomed their first son, Genaro Antonio in July 2021. 

Moreover, she was previously married to Rocky Lachapel in the year 2010 and due to the unhealthiness of their relationship, they got separated in the year 2016. Since that moment they are living their own personal life.
