Overall Meaning

Britnee Kellogg's song "Hey Mama" is an uplifting anthem to all mothers out there who feel overwhelmed with the demands of motherhood. The lyrics of the song are about a mother who is trying her best to juggle all the tasks and responsibilities that come with being a mom, from cleaning up messes to getting dinner ready on time. Despite feeling like she's going to break, the song encourages mothers to take a moment to breathe and find their inner strength. The song acknowledges that motherhood is difficult, but also reminds mothers that they are more than capable of handling it all.


The chorus of "Hey Mama" repeatedly assures mothers that they've "got this," with a child's voice chiming in at the end to encourage their mother even further. The song suggests that mothers have the strength of a hurricane and are fighters in every way. It acknowledges that motherhood can lead to lost identity and feelings of being overwhelmed, but encourages mothers to look around and be proud of everything they've accomplished. Ultimately, "Hey Mama" is a reminder to mothers that they are not alone in feeling these emotions and that they have the strength and resilience to continue on.
