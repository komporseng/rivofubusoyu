Pedro Pascal aka José Pedro Balmaceda Pascal is a Chilean-American actor and model. As of 2023, Pedro Pascal’s net worth is $10 million. He is known for portraying Oberyn Martell in the fantasy drama television series “Game of Thrones” (2011) and Javier Peña in the Netflix Original biographic crime series “Narcos” (2015).

Apart from television shows, he also worked in the films such as The Great Wall, The Equalizer 2, Triple Frontier, Wonder Woman 1984 and We Can Be Heroes. He started his acting career as a guest starring on various American television shows like Good vs. Evil, Downtown and Buffy the Vampire Slayer.

Pedro also appeared in the music video “Fire Meet Gasoline” by Sia. He won the Garland Award and Los Angeles Drama Critics Circle Award for his role in the “International City Theater”.

Pedro Pascal Wiki/Biography

Born on 2 April 1975, Pedro Pascal’s age is 48 Years Old as of 2023. He was born and brought up in a business family from Santiago, Chile, South America. He is holds American and Chilean nationality. He has his belief in the Christian religion.

Pedro Pascal Childhood Pic

He completed his early schooling at Bradford School, Chile. After that, he enrolled himself at the Orange County School of the Arts, California and Tisch School of the Arts, New York from where he completed his acting course.

He was very interested in swimming from an early age and participated in the state championships in Texas at the age of only 11 years. After some time, he quit swimming and start getting drama classes as his interest shifted towards acting.

Career

Pedro Pascal started his career as a television actor. He made his debut appearance on the television screen with the TV serial Good Vs Evil in the year 1999. After that, he has worked in various popular TV shows like “Buffy the Vampire Slayer”.

He rose to popularity after his appearance in the popular TV series Game of Thrones in the fourth season.

Along with that Pascal made his appearance in numerous TV shows and established himself as one of the finest actors in the American television industry.

Along with his television career, Pedro Pascal also made his debut in the film industry with his appearance in the movie Hermanas in the year 2005.
