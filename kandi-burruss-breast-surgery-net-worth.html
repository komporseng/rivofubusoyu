Kandi Burruss Opens About Breasts Reduction Surgery On YouTube Series 'Body Work'

September 2024 · 3 minute read

The Real Housewives of Atlanta star and Xscape member Kandi Burruss shared her breast reduction surgery on her YouTube channel series Body Work. 

Her main objective of sharing is to be real to people, as many celebrities do not publicly share their bodywork.

In the video, she said:

"Let's not let all the other diets and stuff that I've done confuse you into thinking that that's what made my boobs get reduced because it is not, honey!" she said. "I did have some weight loss. I have tried some juice fasts recently. Or, excuse me, 'juice cleanse' recently. Yes, I did lose a couple of pounds with that."

She even admitted that she had lost weight but not for her breast size. She took help from the surgeon to reduce the size of her breast.

Recalling the incident on YouTube, the reality star said that she changed her eating habits by intermittent fasting.

Kandi even shared that she has done some physical changes to her body before. She said:

“As for myself, I think I’ve tried a couple of different things as far as physically changing my body. I’ve had lipo before I got boobs, got a boob reduction, I got another boob reduction, I’ve done a tummy tuck and as far as facial things, I don’t do anything to my face really, but I have tried Botox. Years ago…this was before getting boob implants, I let them take the fat they got from the lipo and put some of it in my boobs.”

In the video shared on 16 August 2021, she also shared some parts of a conversation with the doctor while doing surgery.

Doctor asked her to give her pain medicine. And she asked him, making the room full of laughter, confirming that she wants to have C-cup breasts.
